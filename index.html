How it works

From raw data to independent attribution in minutes

No data scientists. No consultants. No waiting. Upload your spend data and AdCrux delivers attribution results your entire team can act on.

01

Upload your media data

Four columns: week, channel, spend, leads. AdCrux validates, models, and delivers attribution results automatically — no configuration required.

02

The model measures true contribution

AdCrux's MMM engine calculates the actual incremental contribution of each channel — separating real impact from platform overclaiming, organic baseline, and seasonality.

03

Make decisions you can defend

Reallocate budget based on independent data. Present results to clients or leadership with confidence — backed by a model no platform had a hand in building.

6 modules

Six modules. Every attribution question answered.

Each module is designed around a specific decision your marketing team makes every week.

Overview

Executive KPIs, spend by channel over time, and the full performance table with ROAS colour-coding.

Attribution

Model accuracy diagnostics — R², MAPE, Holdout RMSPE — plus actual vs predicted revenue curves.

Saturation

Diminishing returns curves per channel. See which channels are Underinvested, Optimal, or Saturated.

Scenarios

Compare Conservative, Balanced, Aggressive, and Efficiency plans side by side with full revenue projections.

Optimizer

Live budget sliders — drag to reallocate spend and instantly see projected revenue and ROAS impact.

Seasonality

Decompose performance into Trend, Seasonal, and Residual components with a spend index heatmap.
